FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I join the Apocalypse? You can join by either typing /start in a discord server the bot is already in, or invite it to your own server!
How can I view the leaderboard? You can view the leaderboard by navigating to the 'Leaderboard' section on the website. It displays the top players based on their achievements and progress in the game.
What is Discord OAuth? Discord OAuth is a secure way to log in to our website using your Discord account. It allows you to authenticate yourself and access certain features and benefits available to registered users.
How can I join the Discord server? To join our Discord server, click on the 'Join Discord' button and it will redirect you to our server. Make sure you have a Discord account to participate in our community discussions and receive updates about the game.
Can I play the game on mobile devices? Yes, since it is on discord, it can be played on any device.
